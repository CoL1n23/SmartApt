Welcome to SmartApt!

SmartApt is an information management system designed for apartments. Apartment managers usually have to face large quantity of information each day, and this system is developed to ease their jobs.

The following three features are provided.

  • Show Information of Residents
  • Show Information of a Room

    • Registration

  • Register a Resident
  • Register a Student
  • Register an Employee
  • Delete a Resident

Enjoy!

SmartApt utilizes Google Cloud Platform (GCP) service.
